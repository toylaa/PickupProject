Welcome to NP-PickUP!

Welcome to New Paltz Pick Up! We created this site for our Senior Projects course at SUNY New Paltz. The inspiration for the concept came from a meeting with the professor of the course, in which a site to allow sporting events meet-ups was deemed a fresh and fun idea. From personal experience we know what it is like to wish you could find a group of people at your skill level to play a pick-up game of basketball, some touch football, or just skate around with. We hope to allow for others who feel the same to more easily create and attend a group experience.
